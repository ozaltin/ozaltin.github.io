Research

Optimization for Health Systems

My research spans theoretical, computational, and applied aspects of mathematical programming, with emphasis on multilevel stochastic optimization problems in public health policy-making, personalized medical decision-making, and healthcare delivery.

I am also interested in efficient algorithms for large-scale combinatorial problems in bioinformatics and decision-making under uncertainty.
